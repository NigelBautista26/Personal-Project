# SnapNow Business Case
## On-Demand Photography at Your Fingertips

---

# Executive Summary

SnapNow is a mobile-first marketplace connecting travelers with professional photographers at popular destinations worldwide. Built on a proven 20% commission model used by successful platforms like Uber and Airbnb, SnapNow captures a slice of the $37.96 billion photography services market while addressing a clear gap: 78% of travelers struggle to find quality photographers abroad.

**Key Investment Highlights:**
- **$37.96B** global photography services market (2025)
- **1.4 billion** international tourists annually
- **Proven comparable:** Shoott scaled to $10M revenue with just $2.7M funding
- **Capital-efficient model:** Global network effects (like Airbnb, not Uber)
- **Clear path to profitability:** 20% take rate with low variable costs

# Proven Business Model

## How We Make Money

```
Customer pays:         £100
Photographer receives: £80 (80%)
SnapNow earns:         £20 (20%)
```

This mirrors successful marketplace take rates:
- **Airbnb:** 12-15% (guest + host fees)

## Competitive Moat

1. **Network Effects:** More photographers → better coverage → more customers → more photographers
2. **Location Data:** Curated photo spots create unique content moat
3. **Trust & Reviews:** Two-sided rating system builds quality
4. **Mobile-First:** Built for on-the-go travelers (unlike desktop-first competitors)
